About the Artist

Creating on the Bitcoin Blockchain

SpunkArt is a digital artist inscribing original artwork directly onto the Bitcoin blockchain as ordinals. Each piece is a permanent, immutable creation that lives on-chain forever.

From The Damned and Crazy Aliens to Bitcoin Faces and The Freak Show, every collection pushes the boundaries of what digital art on Bitcoin can be. Raw, expressive, and unapologetically bold.

No middlemen. No platforms that can take it down. Just art, permanently inscribed on the most secure blockchain in existence.
