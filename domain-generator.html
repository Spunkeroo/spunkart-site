Tips for Choosing a Great Domain Name

  • 01Keep it short and memorable. Under 12 characters is ideal -- easier to type, spell, and share.
  • 02Avoid hyphens and numbers unless they are part of your brand (like 37signals). They confuse users and hurt word-of-mouth.
  • 03Make it easy to pronounce. If you can't say it out loud clearly, it will be hard for others to remember or search for.
  • 04Check for trademark conflicts. Search the USPTO database and do a quick Google search before you commit.
  • 05Consider .com first, but don't sleep on .io, .co, .dev, or .app -- they signal tech-savviness and are widely accepted.
  • 06Think about social media availability. Check if the matching @handle is free on X, Instagram, and TikTok.
  • 07Avoid trends that will age poorly. Names that feel fresh today can feel dated fast. Go for timeless over trendy.
  • 08Test it with 5 people. Say the name out loud and ask them to spell it. If more than one person gets it wrong, rethink it.
