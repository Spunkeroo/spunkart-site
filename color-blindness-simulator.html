Types of Color Blindness

Protanopia

~1% of males

No red cones. Red appears dark. Difficulty distinguishing red, orange, and green.

Deuteranopia

~1% of males

No green cones. Most common type. Green and red look similar.

Tritanopia

~0.003% of population

No blue cones. Blue appears green. Yellow appears light gray or violet.

Achromatopsia

~0.003% of population

Complete color blindness. Only sees shades of gray. Extremely rare.
