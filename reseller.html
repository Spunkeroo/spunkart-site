FAQ

Can I really sell these as my own? +
Yes. The reseller license gives you full white-label rights. Remove our branding, add yours, sell under your name. We don't appear anywhere in your version.
Is there a revenue share or royalty? +
No. Zero royalties, zero revenue share, zero recurring fees. One-time license purchase and you keep 100% of every sale forever.
Can I modify the code? +
Absolutely. You get full, unminified, commented source code. Add features, change designs, integrate with your systems — it's yours to modify.
How do I protect my resold products? +
The License Server worker is included. Deploy it, generate license keys for your customers, validate on every request. Same system we use.
What about updates? +
Starter: 6 months. Pro: 1 year. Agency: lifetime. We ship new tools monthly — Agency resellers get every new tool automatically.
Can multiple people buy the same reseller tier? +
Yes. Each reseller gets their own license. The market is big enough for everyone. We're focused on making great tools — you focus on selling them.
Do you offer refunds? +
14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the code quality, full refund. But once you start reselling, no refunds (because you've already started profiting).
