Why Use .env Files?

Environment variables keep your API keys, database credentials, and configuration separate from your code. Never commit .env files to git.

  • Add .env to your .gitignore immediately
  • Use .env.example with placeholder values for your team
  • Different .env files for dev, staging, and production
  • Access via process.env.VAR_NAME (Node) or os.environ['VAR_NAME'] (Python)
